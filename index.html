Introduction

Mem (“the App”) respects the privacy of all users. This policy explains how we handle data within the app.

Information We Collect

  • The app automatically creates an account with a random username for each user.
  • No personal information (such as real name, email, phone number) is collected unless voluntarily provided.
  • Only the data of the random account is stored within the app for gameplay purposes (e.g., saving points and progress).

How We Use Information

  • Data is used to facilitate gameplay and save progress within the app.
  • We do not share user data with any third party.
  • We do not sell or trade user information in any form.

Login

Login in the game is automatic and represents a random account only. Users do not need to enter any real personal information.

Security

We use best technical practices to protect the data of random accounts within the app. Other users cannot access your private data within the app.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised with any changes.
